Welcome to Yumin's Taoyuan Journey

Hi, I'm Yumin. This is my personal blog to record the most beautiful memories from the game Taoyuan. It's not a guide or news site, but a place to keep my adventures alive.

About This Blog

Why I started this journey and what you'll find here

I fell in love with Taoyuan because of its peaceful world and beautiful stories. In this game, I found a place where I could escape from daily stress and immerse myself in poetic landscapes and touching narratives.

Every moment in Taoyuan feels special and worth preserving. I created this blog to capture the emotions, the friendships, and the beautiful scenes that made my journey unforgettable. It's my digital memory book.

This isn't a game guide or news site. It's a personal collection of my favorite chapters, important milestones, and the emotions I experienced. Each memory tells a story of discovery, friendship, and wonder in the world of Taoyuan.

Each post is inspired by moments I truly experienced in Taoyuan. I usually combine in-game screenshots, personal reflections, and sometimes creative writing to bring my memories to life in a meaningful way.

This blog is for anyone who loves Taoyuan, enjoys storytelling, or simply wants to relax with some heartfelt experiences. Whether you're a fellow player or just curious, I hope you find joy here.

The Origin of Taoyuan

A wise hermit named Tao Gong planted peach trees with his own hands and lived peacefully in this blessed land.
When people fled from war, he cut down his beloved peach forest to build homes, creating a village where hearts could heal.
Only one sacred peach tree remained, and beside it stands Moon Temple, honoring his gentle soul forever.

My Journey Timeline

Important milestones from my journey.

2023 March-June

The first time I discovered this game. It was a magical moment when I entered the world of Taoyuan for the very first time. I am FuGuang and have one litte sister named TaoTao. We meet a lot of residents in Taoyuan game. Every residents brings us their beautiful story.

2023 July-August

Discovered the joy of dressing up my character with beautiful outfits. My little carrot pet became my sweet companion, and I loved styling both myself and my dear sister TaoTao with lovely clothes that sparkled like dreams.

2023 September-December

Built my own home, my own land, my own island paradise. Every corner was decorated with love and care, creating a peaceful haven where memories bloom like flowers in an eternal spring.

2024 January-March

Met wonderful online friends in this magical world. We shared flowers like gentle whispers, chatted under starlit skies, and found that true friendship blooms across any distance in Taoyuan's embrace.

2024 April-August

Learned to play the piano and unlocked all the beautiful music sheets. Some melodies I played to perfection, earning high achievements that filled my heart with the sweet harmony of accomplishment.

2024 September-December

Discovered many adorable pets - gentle fish, singing birds, and playful cats. Each one brought their own unique charm and whispered different stories, teaching me that every creature has its own magical tale to tell.

2025 January-August

Opened the door to Taoyuan Paradise and met brand new villagers. Each face brought fresh hope and wonder, as if I had discovered a secret garden where new friendships bloom like morning flowers.

2025 September-October

Continue my peaceful farming life, joining joyful events and releasing sky lanterns with wishes. Each glowing lantern carries my heartfelt prayer - "happy every day" - floating toward the stars like dreams taking flight.
